# Phase 15 - Bit Manipulation

## Scope

Bit manipulation is useful for compact state, XOR tricks, and subset-style reasoning.

### Topics

- Bitwise operators
- XOR tricks
- Power of two
- Single number
- Bitmasking
- Subset enumeration
- Bit DP

## Practice direction

- Easy: bitwise basics, power of two
- Medium: single number, XOR tricks
- Hard: subset enumeration, bitmask DP
